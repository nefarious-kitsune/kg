Why this page?

During the Free Transfer period of each season, many players transfer servers. Ideally, players would get permission from the alliances in their destination before the jump. In reality, there is no publicly available information on what server has what alliances, so players would blindly make the jump and then regret it because they land in servers that are dead or hostile.

KGI is a project to publish information on servers over multiple seasons so that players have some usable reference on where to transfer in future Free Transfer periods.
