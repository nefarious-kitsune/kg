Magic Stone

Information on how to acquire Magic Stones (Magic Gems).

See also:

  • Magic Stone Upgrade Cost

Recommendation

Get T2 and T3 Magic Dusts from King/Queen's Hall and Alliance Shop. Wait for higher tier Magic Dusts to become available in events.

Availability

Tier How to acquire
T2 Craft from Tier-2 Magic Dust from King's (Queen's) Hall
T3 Craft from Tier-3 Magic Dust from Alliance Shop
T4 Formerly available through events
T5 Formerly available through the Eve of Expedition event
T6 Formerly available as daily reward in the Mightiest Kingdom event
T7 Formerly available through events
T8 Formerly available as rank reward in the Mightiest Kingdom event
T9 Formerly available as rank reward in the Mightiest Kingdom event
