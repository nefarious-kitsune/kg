Magic Stone Power

The Magic Stone Power depends on Magic Power and Magic Stone power bonus.

Calculation

Note: when calculating Magic Stone Power, the Dark Magic Power is rounded to the nearest tens. For example, if the Dark Magic Power is 216404992, then the value of 216404990 is used to calculate the Magic Stone Power.

Magic Stone Power = ( Light Magic Power + Dark Magic Power ) × Magic Stone power bonus
