Light/Dark Magic Power

The Witch's Lab has Light/Dark Magic Power according to Light and Dark Magic levels.

  • Light and Dark Magic do not need to be balanced.
  • To increase Light Magic level, the player consume Light Reagent (see upgrade cost table).
  • To increase Dark Magic level, the player need to obtain Dark Reagent through PvP battles. Dark Magic level upgrade is automatic.
  • For Dark Magic, partial levelling is possible (e.g. it is possible to have Dark Magic Power between level 1900 and 1901).

Calculation

Light and Dark Magic Powers follow a power table.
