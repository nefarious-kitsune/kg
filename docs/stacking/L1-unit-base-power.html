Unit Base Power

Each troops march can have a certain number of tower defense units. These units contribute to the Unit Base Power.

The Unit Base Power depends on Castle level, Troops capacity, and Unit Specialization.

Calculation

Unit Base Power = Castle Level × Troops Capacity × 10

Stacking Graph

Troops capacity

By default, each troops march has six (6) units. The unit count can be increased by four (4) via Academy Tech. The unit count can be increased by nine (9) via Expedition Tech.

Unit Specialization

With Unit Specialization, your march can gain additional power form units of other elements.

  • To increase Archer power, do Unit Specialization on Fire, Ice, and Goblin.
  • To increase Fire power, do Unit Specialization on Archer, Ice, and Goblin.
  • To increase Ice power, do Unit Specialization on Archer, Fire, and Goblin.
  • To increase Goblin power, do Unit Specialization on Archer, Fire, and Ice.

Example

Example
Castle Level 810
Troops Capacity 9
Default troops capacity 6
1st Troop Capacity Tech (Level 3) +3
2nd Troop Capacity Tech (level 0) +0
Unit Specialization (Archer) +0%
Unit Specialization (Fire) +10%
Unit Specialization (Ice) +20%
Unit Specialization (Goblin) +20%
