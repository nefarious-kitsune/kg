Unit Specialization

With Unit Specialization, your march can gain additional power form units of other elements.

  • To increase Archer power, do Unit Specialization on Flame Mage , Ice Wizard, and Goblin.
  • To increase Flame Mage power, do Unit Specialization on Archer, Ice Wizard, and Goblin.
  • To increase Ice Wizard power, do Unit Specialization on Archer, Flame Mage , and Goblin.
  • To increase Goblin power, do Unit Specialization on Archer, Flame Mage , and Ice Wizard.

Calculation

Calculation for Archer units:

Unit Base Power = Castle Level × Troops Capacity × 10 × 100% + Flame Mage special. + Ice Wizard special. + Goblin special.

Calculation for Flame Mage units:

Unit Base Power = Castle Level × Troops Capacity × 10 × Archer special. + 100% + Ice Wizard special. + Goblin special.

Calculation for Ice Wizard units:

Unit Base Power = Castle Level × Troops Capacity × 10 × Archer special. + Flame Mage special. + 100% + Goblin special.

Calculation for Goblin Mage units:

Unit Base Power = Castle Level × Troops Capacity × 10 × Archer special. + Flame Mage special. + Ice Wizard special. + 100%
