New Player Guide

Information for new players.

Note: I have 2-year experience on S2 but I have little experience with S3. I recently started a new account on S1742 so that I could write a proper guide for new players. Information here may be incomplete or even incorrect, but I'll do my best to guide new players.

What is this game about?

There is no snake shooting (or whatever was in the baiting commercial that you saw). While tower defense is an important part, Kingdom Guard is mainly a war game where players join force in alliances and fight in various wars.

What to expect?

The Fog will gradually lift and allow your alliance to expand in other territory. When the entire map is cleared, alliances will fight for the throne in the Scorched Land at the center of the map. See general timeline.

What should I do?

Things that all new players should do.

  • Join an active alliance as soon as possible. Do not create your own alliance.

    When the server first begins, the system automatically creates several empty alliances with their own Central Fortresses. These alliances will have names like UN13 or BR1. The member who grows the fastest in power can become the leader (R5).

    Again, do not create your own alliance. You need 20 members to start building a Central Fortress, and it's very unlikely you'll get that many. If your region has no active alliance, it's best to patiently wait for the fog to lift and move to a more active region instead.

  • Do Alliance Tech Donation as much as possible

  • Try to complete chapter 1-19 of the Dragon Quest as quickly as possible so you can unlock Dragon.

  • Try to complete five (5) quest levels of Jennifer's Station quickly so you can complete three (3) Gallery Puzzles (required for Dragon Quest).

  • Pass levels and upgrade your castle as quickly as you can. You need to reach Castle Lv. 159 to unlock Academy and do the Academy Research to increase your March Queue from 2 to 3.

  • Get your free Mini Titan avatar frame

  • Join the official Discord server and meet other players. You can ask veteran players for tips.
