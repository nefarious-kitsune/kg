Skin Power New Servers

You can increase your troops power by using Castle Skins, Avatar Frames, and Scenes (unlocked after castle reaches level 2000).

Skins and Frames are either Permanent or Time-Limited.

If you own a Permanent skin, your troops automatically receives the owned stats boost. If you activate a skin, your troops will receive an additional use stats boost.

If you own a Time-Limited skin, your troops do not receives any stats increase until you activate the skin.

See skin activation example.

Get your free Mini Titan avatar frame

  • Visit the KingdomGuard Official Store and log in with your in-game ID and your in-game name.
  • Once logged in, copy the Gift Code displayed on the store page.
  • Go to the Community Treasure Hunt page to redeem your code.
  • The avatar frame will be in your bag. Go to your bag, select the frame, and tap USE.

Get the Kingdom Guard avatar frame

You can obtain time-limited and permanent Kingdom Guard avatar frame by participating in the Power Ranking event, which opens one week after the server is first opened.

If You Have Permanent Skins…

If you have both permanent and time-limited skins, activating the permanent skin(s) will have the same effect as activating the time-limited ones (see example).

Use the permanent skins and save your time-limited skins. The Exchange Shop will be unlocked later, and you can exchange the time-limited items for permanent ones.
