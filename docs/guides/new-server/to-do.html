Things to Do (New Servers)

Things that everyone on new servers should do.

Get your free Mini Titan avatar frame

Follow to steps on the Skin Power page to get your free Mini Titan avatar frame for up to 2% power increase.

Join the Official KG Discord

Join the official Discord server and meet other players. You can ask veteran players for tips.

Get Your Free VIP Points

Go to your bag Bag and redeem the free 60 VIP points. Use the points to upgrade yourself to VIP 2. That will increase your Action Points queue by 50 (the queue will fill up in 15 hours instead of 12 hours).

You can get more VIP points by completing Imperial Treasures I, II, III, and IV.

Donate to Alliance Tech

Alliance Tech is very important in building a strong alliance. Alliance Tech provides buffs such as marching speed, gold gathering, and damage recovery.

If you lose a territory war, you will lose the buffs from the lost altars. However, you will never lose the Alliance Tech (even if you lost the Central Fortress!)

If you are an R4 or R5, send out regular reminder and ask your members to do Tech donation. Mark the tech you want to focus on with a star so that the tech can complete faster.

Buy Desert Castle Skin every day

Get two 1-Day Desert Castle Skin from Alliance Store every day but do not use the skins. (Explain this later).
