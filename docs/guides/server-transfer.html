Free Server Transfer

𝆕𝆔 A Whole New World… 𝆕𝆔

A mysterious portal has just opened. But will it be Gate to Hell or Gate to Heaven? Follow these tips before you enter the portal.

  • Know where you are going. If you don't know anyone there, make an alter ego and scout the server first.
  • Before you move, check the server again. Remember, you are not the only player moving. A rouge player or alliance might have landed in your preferred destination and disrupted server peace with Mithril attack and gold plundering.
  • Refresh alliance quests with gems until they are all SSR (gold). Try not to claim any quest rewards before you move. The quests will not reset and you may be splitting your alliance points among two alliances.
  • Spend all your lava cave points. You won't be able to do lava cave for one week after you transfer. I don’t know if you will get ranking rewards, but spend the points just in case.
  • Discount Store progress will reset, and you won't have time to unlock the store again. Buy any item that you need to buy from the store. If you haven't completed all levels, try to complete the levels before you move.
  • Wishing Crystal Ball resets after you move. Do your free spin before you move, and do it again after you move.
  • Mightiest Kingdom reset after you move. Do a little upgrades and claim the daily reward before you move. Do it again after you move.
  • claim alliance gold before you move.
  • Mithril reset after transfer. Send troops in Mithril mines as soon as you land.
