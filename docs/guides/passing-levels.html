Passing Levels

Some tips on passing tower defense levels.

  • When you go to bed, load the game and keep your phone plugged in to the charger. The game will probably crash at some point, but when you wake up you should have passed a few dozen levels.
  • Put your Ice Wizard at the first tower. The wizard can slow down invading monsters and make them easier to kill.
  • If you are stuck, keep training new units and merge them until all units are at max level.
  • If you still cannot pass levels, upgrade your units at the barrack. Do not exhaust your unit upgrade resources because you might need them in events.
  • If you still cannot pass levels, upgrade your heroes.
  • Do not switch your lead heroes to tower-defense heroes (e.g. Arwyn or Ophelia).
