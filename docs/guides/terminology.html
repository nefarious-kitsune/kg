Terminology

Explanation of terms that you may hear from other Kingdom Guard players:

AP hero
AP-discount hero
A hero with the hero skill of AP reduction and damage recovery from attacking monsters. See Hero Roles.
BoD
Abbreviation for Battle of the Dragon.
Bottom 4 tech
The four Unit Specialization tech listed at the bottom of the Academy.
ESI
Abbreviation for Evil Spirit Invasion.
NAP
Non aggression pact between two alliances, servers, or factions.
Port
The act of destroying a castle. When a castle is destroyed, the system will move (port) the castle to a random location.
S1
The first group of servers (9001~9024) (see Server Groups).
S2
The second group of servers (S401~S1150) (see Server Groups).
S3
The third group of servers (S1120~) (see Server Groups).
