Hero Carry Load

When a hero receives a level upgrade, the hero gains increased Base Power and increased Carrying Load.

Carrying Load is approximately 5000 × (1 + Load Bonus) × (Level).

Level Load Bonus Carrying Load
1 20% 6 000
4 20% 24 000
5 20% 30 000
