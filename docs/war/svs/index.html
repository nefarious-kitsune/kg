Cross-Server Invasion

Cross-Server Invasion is a recurring event that's unlocked after a server enters the SvS Phase.

General Rules

  • It's a one-day event that occurs on Sunday once every two weeks.
  • Three (3) servers are matched up against each other in SvS.
  • If an alliance holds a throne for 8 hours continuously, the alliance wins the throne, and the throne/towers of the server can no longer be attacked.
  • If no alliance is able to hold throne for 8 hours continuously, then the alliance that holds the throne for the longest time wins the throne.

Attack or Defend?

A server can choose to either (1) defend the throne at home, (2) attack the throne of another server, or (3) both.

Winning Home Throne

Servers generally prioritize defending their home thrones.

  • If an alliance successfully wins the home throne, the R5 of the alliance can appoint a player as the King/Queen of the server.
  • The King (Queen) can distribute gift to other players.
  • The King (Queen) can appoint other players as officials, and players who became officials will have certain buffs.
  • The King (Queen) is able to search the location of another player and shackle the player (preventing the player from raising a shield or teleport). The skills enable the King (Queen) enforce server rules by punishing rule breakers.
