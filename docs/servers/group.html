Server Groups

There are three main groups of Kingdom Guard servers, which many players refer to as S1, S2, and S3 servers.

The first group (S1) of servers are numbered 9001 to 9024. The entire group was locked shortly after the second group was opened.

The second group (S2) of servers are numbered 401 to 1150. As of Season 27, the group has around 360 active servers (see S27 Server Status).

The third group (S3) of servers are servers numbered 1120 or above.

Server Differences

S2 and S3 servers have different configurations. Because of this, transfer between the two groups is currently impossible.
