Dragon Nest Battle

The Dragon Nest Battle event starts at approximately day 3, day 16, and day 19. During the event, alliances can occupy Dragon Nests and receives First-Occupation Rewards.

Rules

After the event starts, alliances have 5 days to occupy Dragon Nests for rewards. To occupy a Dragon Nest:

  • The alliance's territory must touch the Dragon Nest.
  • Alliance must attack the defending monsters and defeat them. Each attack requires 8 ~ 10 Action Points (depending on the Hero used).
  • After the monsters are driven out, the alliance must occupy the Dragon Nest for a period of time to claim the Dragon Nest.
  • When the alliance successfully claims the Dragon Nest, every alliance member will receive the First-Occupation Rewards.

Tips

  • Before the event starts, build obelisks so your territory touches the Dragon Nest. If Expedition Fortress is unlocked, build your Expedition Fortress next to the Dragon Nest to save time.
  • Check the defense power of the Dragon Nest. The defending monsters will recover from damage, so you must be able to defeat the monsters quickly. Don't be greedy and attack a Dragon Nest that you cannot successfully claim.
  • Use a Normal Teleport to move your castle close to the Dragon Nest so your marches can go back quickly after each failed attack.
  • To save Action Points, use a 2-star Harold and/or a 1-star Miku if you have them.
