The Great Fog

The following is based on observation by players. The exact mechanism is not public information and may deviate from the description.

In the beginning, the Kingdom is under the Great Fog.

  • Players are initially located in 4 different regions (1, 2, 3, and 4).

    Each region is further divided into 2 sub-regions (A and B)

    See Division Map

  • Players likely fill A sub-regions first and B sub-regions later.

    A new Kingdom likely spawns when all sub-regions are filled.

  • Players cannot move into area that's under the fog from their perspective. Note that regions overlap in some area so players may be able to see each other before the fog lifts.

  • On day 7, the fog will partly lift, and players can cross into the other sub-region of the same region.

  • On day 16, the fog will completely lift, and players can cross into another region.
