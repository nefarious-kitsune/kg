Day 0

Lost

Once again, a few hundred souls got tricked by false commercials and were dropped on the a barren land shrouded by a fog. While exploring the unknown landscape, they found abandoned Fortresses and gathered together to form Alliances. The Lords, as the people liked to call themselves, killed monsters and gathered resources to grow stronger. The ambitious ones who got strong the fastest rose up in ranks and became Alliance leaders.

As the Lords grew tired, some found a magic board called Kingdom Chat. With the magic board, they could talk to others in the fog and shared their knowledge.

The night fell, and the chatter slowly died down. One by one the Lords took rest as another day of adventure awaited them.
