Graphic Assets for fellow Content Creators

One challenge with content creation is that a lot of information has to fit in a limited space. To reduce real estate, graphics can be used to replace text. However, we cannot just take screenshot and crop images to create graphics. The graphics would scale up/down very poorly. Also, some content creators prefer white text on dark background, and graphics may look illegible.

For this Web site, many vector graphics were created to ensure that content will look nice and legible on both desktop and mobile screens. The graphics are listed here so other content creators can easily find them and adapt them into their content.
