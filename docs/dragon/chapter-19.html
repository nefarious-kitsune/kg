Chapter 19

After completing Chapter 19 of the Dragon Quest, the player will be able to hatch the dragon.

Missions

  • Earn 60 daily quest point(s)
  • Independently gather 40K Gold
  • Independently perform Alliance Tech Donation 10 time(s) in total
  • Make 1 purchase(s) in Alliance Store in total
  • Castle reaches Lv. 105

Rewards

  • 1 Dragon Crystal
  • 230 Gems
  • 120 Excellent Dragon Runes
  • 100 Stone
  • 500K Gold
