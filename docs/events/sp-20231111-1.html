Forest Spirit Prayer Festival: Hospitality of the Forest Elder

How to Play

  • The player complete tasks in Hospitality of the Forest Elder to earn Level-1 Token (Clay).
  • New quests are unlocked each day. The player has 7 days to complete all quests (the player does not have to complete the task on the day the tasks are unlocked).
  • The player can use the Level-1 Token (Clay) to play the puzzle game (Pray and Play Elimination) and receive points, and then use the points to redeem Level-2 Token (Life Essence) in Elder's Reciprocation.
  • If the player plays actively, the player should earn enough Level-1 Token (Clay) to get the maximum rewards in Elder's Reciprocation without completing every tasks.
