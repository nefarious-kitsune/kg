Tips for First Occupation of a Dragon Nest

Tips on first-occupation of a Dragon Nest:

  • Before the Dragon Nest unlocks, build obelisks so your territory touches the Dragon Nest. If possible, build your Expedition Fortress next to the Dragon Nest to save time.
  • Check the defense power of the Dragon Nest and make sure you have the power to defeat the monsters within 10 minutes. Don't be greedy and attack a Dragon Nest that you cannot successfully claim.
  • Teleport your castle close to the Dragon Nest so you can return to attack after each failed attempt.
  • To save Action Points, use a 2-star Harold and/or a 1-star Miku if you have them.
