Hero Roles

In Kingdom Guards, each hero has a different set of skills that make them suitable for one of the following roles:

  • Support
  • Warrior
  • Hunter
  • Miner

Lead and Assistant Heroes

Each troops can have one lead hero and up to 2 assistant heroes.

The lead hero will have all skills (e.g. march speed and power bonus) activated.

The assistant hero(s) will only have the power bonus (if available) skill activated.

Role: Support

The two assistant heroes are Support heroes. For them, we are only concerned about their power bonus skill.

Good support heroes include:

  • Archer: Sahar and Ptolemy (25% power bonus)
  • Fire: Cosette and Paul (30% power bonus)
  • Ice: Ao Deng Ge Ri Le (40% power bonus)
  • Goblin: Rosamond (30% power bonus)

Role: Warrior

The lead hero is a Warrior hero if he/she has 3+ of these skills: march speed bonus, power bonus, recovery speed bonus, and regeneration bonus.

Good Warrior heroes include:

  • Archer: Sahar, Meg, and Ptolemy
  • Fire: Ericka and Anko
  • Ice: Ao Yue and Filius
  • Goblin: Gruen and Wendy

Role: Hunter

The lead hero is a Hunter hero if he/she has the AP-reduction bonus. A Hunter hero has the following benefits:

  • Reduce Action Points needed for rallies.
  • Recover all damage from attacking Lava Haka.
  • Recover all damage from attacking Frost Behemoth.
  • Recover all damage from attacking Evil Defenders in Broken Land towers.

Good Hunter heroes include: Livia, Giselle, Jessica, and Pythia.
