Mightiest Kingdom Upgrade Planner FAQ

Why making this tool?

Many players save resources for weeks or even months in order to win the top rewards in the Mightiest Kingdom / Ultimate Power event.

However, even with a lot of resources saved, it is hard for players to know if they would have enough points. If they spend the resources too soon and did not win, the experience would be very painful.

The planner tool helps players estimate how much points they can get from the event and therefore make more informed decisions.

How accurate are the calculated results?

I do not have all the data on the upgrade costs of Witch's Lab and Blacksmith. Nevertheless, I believe the calculated result should be fairly close to the actual result.
