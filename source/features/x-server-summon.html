Information from the developer on the feature is fairly sketchy. The following are based on observation by players and may not reflect the actual mechanics of the feature.

Overview

During the Free Server Transfer week, alliances on some selected servers may recruit players from other servers via Alliance Cross-Server Summon.

Eligibility

Based on observation by players

  • Generally servers with relatively low activity level are qualified.
  • Qualified servers are randomly selected each season.
  • If a servers is selected, the server won't be selected the next season.

Summoning

If a server is selected for the feature, alliances in that servers may use the Cross-Server Summon feature

Based on observation by players

  • Only alliances that qualify to enter Burning Expedition can use the feature.
  • Only R5 can post a summon on the summoning board. The summon includes information such as the nature of alliance (farming, casual, or fighting) and minimum player power.
  • Players can only see summons from other servers (they cannot see summons posted by their own server).
  • Players apply to join the alliances that posted the summons. All applications requires approval by the R5 of the summoning alliance.
  • A summon cannot be cancelled after posting.
