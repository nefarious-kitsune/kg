Overview

  • Everyone starts with 12 Attacks and 12 Moves
  • 1 Move is needed to remove a slate to uncover monsters, gate, and boulders
  • 15 Moves are needed to remove a boulder
  • 2 Attacks are needed to defeat a Normal Monster and deal 4 Damages against Boss Haka
  • 5 Attacks are needed to defeat a Elite Monster and deal 15 Damages against Boss Haka
  • The Gate is always at the top row of the map. When the Boss is defeated, the Gate will unlock and you can proceed to the next stage. Going through the Gate does not require any Moves
