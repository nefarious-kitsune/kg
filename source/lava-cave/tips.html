Hit the small monsters first

When you defeat the Normal and Elite monsters, the Haka Boss will take additional damage. Concentrate on the small monsters first so that you can defeat the Haka Boss more efficiently.

Uncover Haka Boss last

If you have the Cave Map and can see through tiles, leave Haka Boss as the last thing you uncover. When you uncover something, all neighboring tiles become unlocked, and you could accidentally lock up small monsters.

Watch the Exit

Sometimes the Exit is blocked by many Boulders and Haka Boss is defeated before the Exit is uncovered. If you see that there is no red cross sign on the Exit Exit, it means the Exit is already open, so proceed to the next stage.

Do not hit free boulders

As the stage level increases, so will Haka Boss's health point. When you are at very high level, you might get frustrated that you have 12 Moves but no Attack. Do not take your frustration out on the free Boulders. Lava Cave is about team work, and you do not want to send the wrong message.

Give direction

R4 and R5 can set flags on the map to help members on deciding how to use their Moves/Attacks.

Also, send out regular reminder mails on Lava Cave. You may use pre-written mail templates.
