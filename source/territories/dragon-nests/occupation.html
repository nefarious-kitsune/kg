To occupy and claim a Dragon Nest:

  • The Alliance's territory must touch the Dragon Nest.

  • If there are defenders in the Dragon Nest, the alliance need to attack the Dragon Nest to drive out the defenders.

  • After the defenders are driven out, the Alliance need to garrison the Dragon Nest for a period of time to claim the Dragon Nest. Each member can send multiple troops marches to a Dragon Nest.

  • When the alliance successfully claims the Dragon Nest, members of the Alliance may receive rewards.

First-Occupation

Dragon Nests open at approximately Day-3, Day-16, and Day-19.

Dragon Nests are initially guarded by monsters. To claim a Dragon Nest, the Alliance must attack the Dragon Nest and drive out the defending monsters. Each attack requires 8-10 Action Points (depending on the Hero used). After the first attack, the Alliance must defeat the monsters within 10 minutes, otherwise the Dragon Nest will automatically reset to its initial condition.

See first-occupation tips and occupation time.

Second-Occupation

If a Dragon Nest is owned by another alliance, the alliance need to attack the Dragon Nest to drive out all garrisoning troops and then occupy the Dragon Nest for a period of time When the alliance successfully claims the Dragon Nest, all stored gold will be distributed to all participating members.
