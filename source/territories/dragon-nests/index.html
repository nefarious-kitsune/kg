Each Alliance may claim a maximum of eight (8) Dragon Nests. Each owned Dragon Nest will provide the Alliance some buffs:

Benefits of Owning Dragon Nests

  • Power Increase: Each Dragon Nest has an associated Element (Archer, Fire, Ice, or Goblin), and it provides the Alliance an power increase to all troops of the element.

    For example, if an Alliance owns a level-5 Archer Dragon Nest, then every Archer troops in the Alliance receives a 5% power increase.

    The power increases apply regardless of where the Castle is (in Normal Land, invading during Cross-Server Invasion, or in Broken Land).

  • Member Limit Increase: Owned Dragon Nests can increase the member count limit of the Alliance.

  • Bonus Gold: Gold mined by Alliance members are stored at 1:1 ratio in the nest, and stored gold are distributed to all members at the end of each week.
