An example of Troops Marches for a F2P player after about 2 weeks of playing. — by Kitsune21

March 1

Miner
 
 
 

Used to mine gold:

  • 4-star Kris has bonus Gathering Speed and bonus March Speed.
  • Kris and Ophelia get the most level upgrades so that they have more carrying load and can stay in a Dragon Nest longer

March 2

Hunter
 
 
 

Used to start Titan rallies:

  • 2-star Harold has Action-Point discount skill, so he can help collect more resources from Titan rallies and Jennifer's Station quests.
  • Arwyn is currently a filler here.

March 3

Builder
 
 
 

Used to build obelisks:

  • 1-star Gabriel has bonus March Speed.
  • Alucard is currently a filler here.
