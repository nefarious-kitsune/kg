Example of Troops Marches of a F2P player after a few months (~3 months) of playing. — by Kitsune21

March 1

Miner
 
 
 

Used to mine gold:

  • 4-star Kris has bonus Gathering Speed and bonus March Speed.
  • Ophelia and Samar both have Unit Power buff.
  • These heroes get the most level upgrades so that they have more carrying load and can stay in a Dragon Nest longer

March 2

Hunter
 
 
 

Used to start Titan rallies:

  • 3-star Harold has Action-Point discount skill.
  • Gabriel is currently a filler here.
  • 3-Star Arwyn has Unit Power buff.

March 3

Hunter
 
 
 

Used to start Titan and Lava Haka rallies:

  • Jessica has better March Speed than Harold, and she has Action-Point discount skill buff and can recover damage from Lava Haka rallies.
  • Merlin and Hadi are fillers here.
